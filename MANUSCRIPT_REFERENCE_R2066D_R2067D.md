# Manuscript Reference — R2066D/R2067D

R2066D/R2067D correspond to the transition from witness-checker execution to a first concrete local-discharge closure and then to certificate export.

R2066D is the first layer in this branch that may record the first concrete obligation as locally discharged, because it carries a proof of `AR.localDischargeConclusion`.

R2067D exports the checked certificate and links it to the endpoint branch.

Non-claims remain explicit: these layers do not prove the endpoint theorem, RH, or a full RH formalization.
