# R1968D Seventh Best Step

The seventh step after R1967D is to classify each source-evidence slot by its expected formal anchor type.

This is the best next step because the remaining analytic work is no longer a single undifferentiated block.  R1968D separates it into:

1. existing-library or mathlib-style anchors,
2. external classical theorem anchors,
3. manuscript theorem anchors,
4. new analytic formalization anchors,
5. audit/ledger anchors.

This lets later work focus on the truly hard part: the slots classified as `newAnalyticFormalizationAnchor`, especially the Li-kernel, Abel-boundary, packet-object, packet-estimate, endpoint, and little-oh components.
